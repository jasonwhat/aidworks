Why Aid Works

Saves Lives

Every dollar spent on foreign aid translates to measurable improvements in health, education, and economic opportunity for the world's most vulnerable populations. The data is clear: aid works.

Builds Security

Stable, prosperous communities don't become breeding grounds for conflict, terrorism, or mass migration. Aid creates the conditions for peace and security worldwide.

Creates Prosperity

Countries that receive aid become trading partners and allies. When the world prospers, Canada prospers. It's an investment in our shared future and economic growth.

Reflects Our Values

Canada's leadership in international development reflects who we are as a nation: compassionate, forward-thinking, and committed to justice and human dignity.
